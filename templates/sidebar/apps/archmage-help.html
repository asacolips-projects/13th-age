Weapons

Weapons are broken down into melee (@wpn.m), ranged (@wpn.r), JAB (@wpn.j), PUNCH (@wpn.p), and KICK (@wpn.k). JAB, PUNCH, and KICK are intended for monks, and will use the monk damage die (d6, d8, d10, respectively) and will use Dexterity for attacks and Strength for damage.

  1. Weapon Damage Die (single)

    @wpn.m.die @wpn.r.die @wpn.j.die @wpn.p.die @wpn.k.die

  2. Weapon Damage Dice (per level)
