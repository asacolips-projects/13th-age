The following stats can all be used in inline or regular dice rolls.

Example attack: [[d20+@str.mod+@std+@atk.m.bonus]] vs. AC

Example damage: [[@wpn.m.dice+@str.dmg+@atk.m.bonus]] slashing damage

Ability Modifiers

  1. @str.mod @dex.mod @con.mod @int.mod @wis.mod @cha.mod

Ability Modifiers (x1 Adventurer, x2 Champion, x3/x4 Epic 1e/2e)

  1. @str.dmg @dex.dmg @con.dmg @int.dmg @wis.dmg @cha.dmg

Note: for multiclass characters the above are affected by the Key Modifier. For class powers that's fine, but should you need to access the unaltered modifiers you can use e.g. @str.nonKey.mod or @str.nonKey.dmg.

Item Attack / Damage Bonuses

  1. Melee, Ranged, Arcane, Divine

    @atk.m.bonus @atk.r.bonus @atk.a.bonus @atk.d.bonus

Resources

Resources (Command Points, Focus, Ki, Momentum) can be accessed via the @rsc shorthand, as shown here below. For ki and custom resources, a max suffix shorthand is also provided.

  1. @rsc.cps @rsc.focus @rsc.ki @rsc.kimax @rsc.momentum

Note: any custom resources that is both enabled and has a label set can be accessed with that label, reduced to lowercase letters only without spaces (e.g. a resource called "Power Points" can be accessed via @rsc.powerpoints and @rsc.powerpointsmax).

Weapons

Weapons are broken down into melee (@wpn.m), ranged (@wpn.r), JAB (@wpn.j), PUNCH (@wpn.p), and KICK (@wpn.k). JAB, PUNCH, and KICK are intended for monks, and will default to the monk damage die (d6, d8, d10, respectively).

  1. Weapon Damage Die (single)

    @wpn.m.die @wpn.r.die @wpn.j.die @wpn.p.die @wpn.k.die

  2. Weapon Damage Dice (per level)

    @wpn.m.dice @wpn.r.dice @wpn.j.dice @wpn.p.dice @wpn.k.dice
